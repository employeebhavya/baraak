Baraak by BVK is a celebration of flavor, heritage, and hospitality—bringing together the rich culinary traditions of India and Arabia in the heart of Dubai. Founded by the Kannan Ravi Group, a reputed business conglomerate in the UAE, Baraak opened its doors on May 26, 2024, on Sheikh Khalifa Bin Zayed Road, Al Karama. The restaurant’s grand launch was a star-studded affair, graced by leading South Indian film stars and dignitaries including MPs Kathir Anand and Thol Thirumavalavan, and Tambaram Deputy Mayor Kamaraj, among others.

With a menu curated to reflect the soul of Indo-Arabian cuisine, Baraak blends age-old recipes with contemporary presentation, offering a unique dining experience for food lovers across Dubai. The ambiance is designed to exude warmth and authenticity, making it an inviting space for families, friends, and culinary explorers.

Backed by the trusted legacy of the Kannan Ravi Group, Baraak is more than just a restaurant—it is a cultural bridge and a new benchmark in fusion dining.
