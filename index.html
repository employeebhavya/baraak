Our Story

Kannan Ravi Group, a prominent UAE-based business conglomerate founded by visionary entrepreneur Kannan Ravi from Tamil Nadu, has been at the forefront of diversified industries since 1995, including electromechanical services, real estate, and technical contracting. With a strong presence across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, and Fujairah, the Group continues to expand its portfolio with innovation and integrity. In a landmark move into the food and beverage industry, the Group launched its flagship Indo-Arabian restaurant, Baraak, on May 26, 2024, in Al Karama, Dubai. This high-profile opening, attended by distinguished political figures and celebrated film personalities, marks a bold new chapter in the Group’s journey—blending cultural flavors and entrepreneurial excellence.
